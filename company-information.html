About Us

Our Journey Since 2003

22+
Years Experience

Founded in 2003, our company began its journey in the used car export business. In the early years, we primarily exported vehicles to Russia, Pakistan, the UK, UAE, and other international markets.

Over time, we diversified into the used automotive parts industry. Today, we specialize in bulk export of used auto parts to the Philippines, Pakistan, UAE, Russia, Africa, and Latin America.
