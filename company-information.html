Your Complete Export Solution for Japanese Vehicles & Parts

Established in 2003, we began our journey as a dedicated exporter of used vehicles. In our early years, we built a strong reputation serving markets in Russia, Pakistan, the UK, and the UAE. Over time, we strategically expanded our expertise into the automotive parts sector. Today, we specialize in the bulk export of used vehicles, auto parts, and heavy machinery directly from Japan to a diverse global market, including:

  • CIS Region: Russia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Georgia, and Tajikistan
  • Asia: UAE, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Myanmar, and Sri Lanka
  • Oceania & Europe: Australia, New Zealand, the UK, and greater Europe
  • Africa: Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, South Africa, and Zimbabwe
  • Latin Americas: Jamaica, Bahamas, Barbados, Trinidad & Tobago

YYour All-in-One Partner for Japanese Vehicle & Parts Export

At Biwako Int Trading, we go beyond standard exports. While others only ship vehicles or parts, we provide a complete, one-stop solution tailored to your specific needs. Whether you need a road-ready car or specialized dismantling, we customize every order. Our expert services include:

  • Complete Vehicle Export: Shipping fully functional units worldwide.
  • Precision Dismantling: We can provide Half-cuts, Nose-cuts, or specific high-value parts collections.
  • Advanced Shipping Solutions: We offer Rack and Stack loading to maximize container space.
  • CKD (Completely Knocked Down): Full disassembly down to the last nut and bolt for easy reassembly at your destination.
