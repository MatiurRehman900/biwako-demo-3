  • What types of car parts do you export?
    We export engines, transmissions, body parts, half-cuts, nose-cuts, and complete vehicle dismantling parts for various car makes and models.
    Do you provide quality assurance for parts?
    Yes, all parts are thoroughly inspected and tested before export. We ensure quality standards are met for every shipment.
    Which countries do you export to?
    We export to over 50 countries worldwide, including Africa, Asia, Middle East, and other international markets.
    Can I order specific car parts or models?
    Absolutely. We source specific parts based on your requirements from auctions, suppliers, and our extensive inventory.
    What is half-cut and nose-cut?
    Half-cut is the front half of a vehicle including engine and transmission. Nose-cut is the front section with bumper, lights, and radiator assembly.
  • How do I place an order?
    Contact us with your requirements. We'll provide a quote, confirm availability, and process your order after payment settlement.
    How long does shipping take?
    Shipping time varies by destination. Typically 2-6 weeks depending on the port and customs clearance process.
    Can I track my container?
    Yes, we provide tracking information and regular updates on your container's location and estimated arrival time.
    What container loading methods do you use?
    We offer full car loading, CKD (Completely Knocked Down), rack & tack, and bulk parts loading based on your needs.
    How are parts packed for shipping?
    All parts are professionally packed with protective materials to ensure they reach you in perfect condition.
