Export Business Flow

1

Receiving Orders

We get customer orders and check all details to make sure everything is correct.

2

Arranging Cars & Parts

We collect the required cars and auto parts from auctions, suppliers, or our stock.

3

Dismantling & Cutting

Our skilled team carefully dismantles cars and performs half-cut, nose-cut, or full disassembly as needed.

4

Packing

All parts are packed safely to reach the customer in perfect condition.

5

Container Loading

We load cars and parts into containers using methods like full car loading, CKD, rack & tack, or bulk parts, depending on the customer's needs.

6

Shipment

We handle shipping and all documentation, making sure the container reaches the customer safely and on time.
